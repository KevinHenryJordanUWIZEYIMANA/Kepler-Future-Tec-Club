Apply to Join a Kepler College Club

We are excited that you are interested in joining one of our vibrant student clubs at Kepler College! Please fill out the application form below to get started on your journey of extracurricular engagement and personal growth.

Application Instructions

  1. Fill out all required fields in the application form.
  2. Select the club you wish to join from the dropdown menu.
  3. Provide a brief explanation of your motivation for joining the club.
  4. Click the "Submit Application" button to send your application.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once your application has been received.

If you have any questions or need assistance with the application process, please contact us at info@keplercollege.edu
